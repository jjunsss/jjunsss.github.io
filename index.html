Junsu Kim

Combined MS-PhD · UNIST AI Graduate School

I started my AI journey in 2023, and I am a Combined MS/PhD student in the AI Graduate School (AIGS) at UNIST, advised by Prof. Seungryul Baek. My research spans computer vision, continual learning, open-world object detection, and vision-language models, with a particular focus on integrating these into robotics and foundation models such as diffusion models.

Previously, I interned at NAVER AI Lab, mentored by Dongyoon Han, and served as an NVIDIA DLI Ambassador.
